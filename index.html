I am a PhD student in the Natural Language Processing Group, University of Sheffield, under the supervision of Prof. Nikolaos Aletras and Dr. Nafise Sadat Moosavi. Previously, I earned an MSc. in Data Analytics with distinction from University of Sheffield and B.E. in Economic Statistics from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, China. My research interests are efficient transformer-based language models, as well as transfer learning and domain adaption etc.
